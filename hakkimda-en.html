Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Berk Yağız KALAFAT. As a senior university student, I've fully focused on the limitless possibilities offered by artificial intelligence, machine learning, and modern software development. For me, technology isn't just about writing code; it's a way to build the future and find elegant solutions to complex problems.

My engineering and global vision foundations actually go way back. Starting with a language school in the UK during middle school, followed by a student exchange program in Germany during high school. Also in high school, joining the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team gave me an early introduction to robotics, engineering discipline, and the culture of teamwork.

I don't like limiting myself to just technical skills. I share my insights on tech philosophy, my learnings, and modern software practices like "vibe-coding" through my articles on Medium. When I step away from the screen, I draw inspiration from different fields. Indie games with strong narratives and the indie/rock music scene keep my creativity alive. In my free time, I'm learning to play the electric guitar, capturing moments through photography, and exploring new melodies.

Combining my global experiences and technical background, my biggest goal is to advance my career in the United States. With my free-spirited and innovative approach, I will continue to push my boundaries.
